FMR

Faithful Medical Reasoning

Measure · Correct · Abstain — a faithfulness layer for medical VLMs

—

Overview

Medical AI that "thinks out loud" sounds trustworthy — but its reasoning often isn't looking at the scan, and the more it reasons, the worse this gets. FMR measures whether each reasoning step is grounded in the image, corrects drift, and abstains — deferring to a clinician — when it cannot be verified, with a distribution-free guarantee.
