Overview

Medical AI that "thinks out loud" sounds trustworthy — but its reasoning is often not actually looking at the scan, and the more it reasons, the worse this gets. FMR measures whether each reasoning step is grounded in the image, corrects it when it drifts, and abstains — deferring to a clinician — when faithfulness cannot be verified, with a distribution-free guarantee.

Claim A · Diagnosis

Medical reasoning VLMs lose visual groundedness as the reasoning chain grows.

Claim B · Safety

A calibrated, faithfulness-triggered abstention gate is a new safety layer for that failure mode.

The Diagnosis — does grounding decay along the reasoning chain?

Measurement — the multi-signal Faithfulness Score

Three independent signals — A counterfactual sensitivity, B attention grounding, C self-consistency — are fused into one score. The fusion is designed to beat any single signal.

Robustness & Ablations

Honesty notes. Numbers shown for the Mock (synthetic) source validate the machinery on synthetic data (labeled as such); empirical claims come from the real-model source.
